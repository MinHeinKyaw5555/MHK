Meet-the-roaster

Alex – Head Roaster & Coffee Curator

September 20, 2025

With over a decade of roasting experience blending tradition and innovation, Alex leads our roastery with a passion for flavor exploration. Always experimenting with roast profiles, Alex specializes in uncovering the subtleties in single-origin beans—from bright, floral African varieties to rich, chocolatey Central American coffees.
